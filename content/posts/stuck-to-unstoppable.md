## Stuck Is Not a Strategy Problem

When people come to me feeling stuck, they often think they need a better plan. A cleaner roadmap. More information about the market, the options, the alternatives.

What they actually need is a different story about who they are.

Stuck is almost never a strategy problem. It's an identity problem.

## The Identity Trap

Here's how it happens. You spend years building competence in a field. You become known for something. You become good at something. And slowly, almost imperceptibly, that thing becomes who you are.

Your title becomes your identity. Your salary becomes your self-worth. Your company becomes your community.

And then one day you realise you want to leave — or change, or grow — and you can't. Not because of logistics. Because leaving feels like losing yourself.

That's the identity trap.

## The Shift That Unlocks Everything

The True Self Unpack is built around one fundamental shift: moving from a fixed identity ("I am a Finance Director") to a fluid one ("I am someone who is capable of building, leading, and creating value — in many forms").

This sounds simple. It is not easy.

It requires dismantling beliefs that have been reinforced for years. Beliefs like:

- "I'm too specialised to do anything else."
- "I've come too far to start over."
- "Success in one thing means I should stay in that thing."

These beliefs are understandable. They are also cages.

## What Unstoppable Looks Like

Unstoppable doesn't mean fearless. It means moving forward with fear present — because your clarity about who you are is stronger than your uncertainty about what comes next.

When you know your values, you can make decisions.  
When you know your strengths, you can make moves.  
When you know your identity, nothing can stop you from building toward it.

## The First Step

The first step is the hardest: admitting that the version of you that got here is not the same version of you that needs to go forward.

That's not failure. That's growth.
